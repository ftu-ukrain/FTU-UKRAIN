📜About FTU Ukraine:

FTU Ukraine is a grassroots football team that connects people from all backgrounds through the power of sport, shared values, and Ukrainian culture. At open events, we promote positivity, unity, and cultural pride — not politics.

🎤When we sing, we sing for hope.🎤

⚽When we play, we play for community.⚽

💙When we gather, we build bridges.💙
